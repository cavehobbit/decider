It's a decision making app for when you can't choose between things.
 Simply pick the best ones and write them in the boxes and let it give you results.
Add your options and hit the button to start, it also has a sound and confetti animation which plays everytime an option is selected. 
It auto saves your options in Local Storage. You can also remove options and mute sounds.

Built with JS, CSS, HTML and uses Web Audio API(for sound).
